# Prenatal & Postpartum Partner Checklist

Welcome! This site is built to support your pregnancy and postpartum journey with simple checklists and guidance. Use the pages below to track progress and act with calm confidence.

- [Labor](labor/)
- [Birth](birth/)
- [Postpartum](postpartum/)
- [Diapers](diapers/)

## Quick checklist

### Pre-labor readiness
- [ ] Confirm birth team contacts
- [ ] Stock snacks, hydration, comfort tools
- [ ] Prepare sleep/birth environment
- [ ] Pack birth and postpartum bag

### First 24 hours postpartum
- [ ] Keep labored person hydrated and nourished
- [ ] Place baby skin-to-skin
- [ ] Track feedings and diapers
- [ ] Rest when possible, partner handles short tasks

> The goal here is clinical planning with a calm, comfortable layer. The core guidance is in the linked pages above.
